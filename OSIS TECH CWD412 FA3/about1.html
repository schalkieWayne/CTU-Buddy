About us

At CTU-Buddy, our mission is to provide a user-friendly and efficient platform that empowers CTU students to excel academically and engage actively within the campus community.

We understand the challenges that students face – from managing assignments and accessing important documents to connecting with faculty and peers.

That's why we have meticulously designed and developed this website to address these pain points and offer a seamless experience.

Main challenge

The inception of CTU-Buddy can be traced back to a shared frustration among our founding members.

As students ourselves, we often found it cumbersome to manage the various documents, assignments, and resources required throughout our academic pursuits.

We recognized the need for a centralized platform that could streamline these processes and enhance communication within the campus community.

Our brainstorming sessions, fueled by late-night study sessions and countless cups of coffee, led us to envision a comprehensive website that would revolutionize student life on campus.

The idea was simple yet powerful: create a virtual "buddy" that students could rely on for quick access to document uploads, announcements, and a direct line of communication with university departments.

Our Mission

At CTU-Buddy, our mission is to provide a user-friendly and efficient platform that empowers CTU students to excel academically and engage actively within the campus community. We understand the challenges that students face – from managing assignments and accessing important documents to connecting with faculty and peers. That's why we have meticulously designed and developed this website to address these pain points and offer a seamless experience.

Key Features

1.Digital Docs: Say goodbye to paper clutter! Upload, store, and retrieve your academic materials effortlessly with CTU-Buddy.

2.Quick Connect: Our "Contact Us" page is a direct portal to faculty, departments, and support. Have a question or feedback? Reach out with ease.

3.Event Hub: Stay updated on CTU events, from insightful seminars to vibrant gatherings, ensuring you're always in the loop.

4.Course Forums: Engage in subject-centric forums. Share insights, seek answers, and collaborate on academic quandaries.

5.Resource Tracker: Bookmark and quickly access essential academic tools and resources tailored to your courses and interests.

6.Notifications**: Get real-time alerts on grades, assignment due dates, and university announcements, keeping you always informed.

Hop on for a simplified, yet enriched CTU-Buddy journey!
