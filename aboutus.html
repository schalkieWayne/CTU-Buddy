About us

At CTU-Buddy, our mission is to provide a user-friendly and efficient platform that empowers CTU students to excel academically and engage actively within the campus community. We understand the challenges that students face – from managing assignments and accessing important documents to connecting with faculty and peers. That's why we have meticulously designed and developed this website to address these pain points and offer a seamless experience.

Key Features

The inception of CTU-Buddy can be traced back to a shared frustration among our founding members. As students ourselves, we often found it cumbersome to manage the various documents, assignments, and resources required throughout our academic pursuits. We recognized the need for a centralized platform that could streamline these processes and enhance communication within the campus community. Our brainstorming sessions, fueled by late-night study sessions and countless cups of coffee, led us to envision a comprehensive website that would revolutionize student life on campus. The idea was simple yet powerful: create a virtual "buddy" that students could rely on for quick access to document uploads, announcements, and a direct line of communication with university departments.
